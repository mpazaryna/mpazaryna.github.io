The Teacher Training Study Group's (TTSG) goal is to provide a space for a peer-led group of current YTT students to practice teach, participate in discussions, and share information related to the yoga teacher training experience. TTSG will come together in a supportive and nurturing environment where participants can hone their teaching cues, sequencing, hands-on adjustments, and voice among peers. The group will follow a code of conduct and etiquette.

TTSG will be a part of the studio's regular schedule, and it does not conflict with the scheduling of other studio classes and seminars. TTSG will only open to other teacher training enrollees to minimize liability and participants' need to carry personal liability insurance. TTSG will include practice sessions, discussions, and peer review topics. In addition to formal, in front of the group experiences, the TTSG would set aside part of the time to allow students to partner with others to do 1:1 or small group-led activities. The small group would enable the participant to gain additional time spent in a hands-on scenario.

TTSG will determine its flow and organization of segments. A TTSG facilitator will be responsible for coordinating the session through sign-up sheets and direction, not lead the entire session. The facilitator will be responsible for the studio's care and the opening and closing of the studio.
